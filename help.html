Frequently Asked Questions

What is VA Hub?

VA Hub is a platform designed to provide affordable and accessible skill development courses for individuals looking to improve their career prospects, especially as virtual assistants.

How do I sign up?

Click on the "Sign Up with Email" button on the main page, fill in your details, and follow the instructions to create your account.

What courses are available?

We offer courses in email management, customer support, graphic design, and more. Check the Courses page for details.

Can I access the courses for free?

Some courses are free, while others require a small fee to access. We strive to keep all courses affordable.
